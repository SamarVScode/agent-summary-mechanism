# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Internal company agents and administrative staff.

## Product Purpose
A dual-facing system for agents to log daily work, request leaves, and view their history, alongside an admin dashboard to review, approve, and manage leave requests. Success is defined by fast, error-free data entry and clear status visibility.

## Brand Personality
Professional, efficient, reliable, clear.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, dated enterprise software (too much noise).
- Consumer marketing pages (too flashy/distracting).

## Design Principles
- The tool should disappear into the task.
- Clear, unambiguous status indicators.
- Consistent affordances across agent and admin views.
- Information density tailored for scanning.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance for contrast. Clear focus states for keyboard navigation. No critical information conveyed by color alone.
